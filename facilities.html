🏢 Facilities

🏢 Facilities

State-of-the-art learning environment

ELIDENS features modern facilities designed for 21st-century learning. Our 25-acre campus includes specialized learning spaces, sports amenities, and technology integration throughout.

Science Laboratories

Three dedicated labs (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) with modern equipment including microscopes, spectrometers, and dissection stations. Safety-certified for all experiments.

  • 20-station capacity each
  • Digital lab management system
  • Weekly science club use

Technology Center

60 modern workstations with high-speed internet, robotics lab, and maker space. Coding curriculum from grade 1 through advanced AP Computer Science.

  • 1:1 device program
  • 3D printing & laser cutting
  • VR/AR learning stations

Learning Resource Center

25,000+ volume library with digital databases, research stations, and quiet study areas. Open 7 days/week with certified librarians.

  • Individual study carrels
  • Group collaboration rooms
  • eBook & audiobook access

Sports & Fitness Complex

Indoor gymnasium, fitness center, 2 basketball courts, soccer field, track. Daily physical education required for all grades.

  • Certified athletic trainers
  • Team & individual sports
  • Adaptive programs available

Performing Arts Center

Theater with 300 seats, music rooms, art studios, dance studio. Annual productions, concerts, and art exhibitions.

  • Professional lighting/sound
  • Orchestra & band programs
  • Visual arts gallery

Outdoor Learning

Organic garden, nature trail, environmental lab. Sustainability education integrated across curriculum.

  • Student-led garden club
  • Outdoor classroom
  • Recycling/composting programs
