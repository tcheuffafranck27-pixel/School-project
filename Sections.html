🏫 Our Sections

Discover the different sections and grades at ELIDENS

Primary Section

Grades: 1-5

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. Our primary section focuses on making learning fun and engaging for young learners.

  • Small class sizes
  • Play-based learning
  • Sports and arts
  • Character development

Middle Section

Grades: 6-8

A crucial phase of development where students explore diverse interests and build deeper understanding of subjects. Emphasis on independent learning and social skills.

  • Subject-specific classes
  • Team projects
  • Leadership programs
  • Science labs

High Section

Grades: 9-12

Advanced academic preparation for college and career success. Students choose specialized streams and prepare for national and international examinations.

  • Advanced curriculum
  • Career counseling
  • Research projects
  • International programs

Academic Excellence Across All Sections

At ELIDENS, we maintain consistent standards of excellence across all sections. Each grade level is designed to build upon previous learning while preparing students for the next phase of their educational journey.

Our experienced faculty members work collaboratively to ensure smooth transitions between sections and to support the holistic development of every student.
