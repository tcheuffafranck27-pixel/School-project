🏫 Our Sections

Discover the different sections and grades at ELIDENS

College Section

Forms: 1-5 (Ages 11-16)

Lower secondary education following Cameroon GCE Ordinary Level curriculum (bilingual English/French streams). Core subjects: Mathematics, Sciences, English, Français, History/Geography, preparing for GCE O-Level.

  • Bilingual (English/French) Instruction
  • GCE O-Level Exam Preparation
  • STEM & Language Focus
  • Modern Science Laboratories
200 Students 95% GCE O-Level Success Rate

High School Section

Forms: Lower Sixth & Upper Sixth (GCE Advanced Level)

Upper secondary GCE Advanced Level curriculum with streams: Sciences, Commerce, Arts. Prepares for GCE A-Level exams and university entrance.

  • GCE O/A Level Preparation
  • Career Guidance & Streams
  • University Entrance Coaching
  • Bilingual Proficiency
250 Students 92% GCE A-Level Pass

Academic Excellence Across All Sections

At ELIDENS, we maintain consistent standards of excellence across all sections. Each grade level is designed to build upon previous learning while preparing students for the next phase of their educational journey.

Our experienced faculty members work collaboratively to ensure smooth transitions between sections and to support the holistic development of every student.
