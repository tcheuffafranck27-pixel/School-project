Admission Process

Online Application

Complete our secure online form. No application fee.

Meet our admissions team (30 mins).

4

Offer & Enrollment

Receive offer within 48 hours. Secure your place!

Requirements by Section

Collège (Forms 1-5)

  • Previous school reports (last 2 terms)
  • Entrance test in Maths/English/Français
  • Birth certificate copy
  • 2 passport-sized photos

High School (Form 5, Lower/Upper Sixth)

  • GCE Ordinary Level results (for Form 5)
  • GCE Ordinary Level results or entrance exam (for Lower Sixth)
  • Interview with Principal
  • Previous transcripts

Important Dates & Fees

2025/2026 Admissions (September Start)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, merit and need-based scholarships available. 30% of students receive assistance.

What is the class size?

Average 18 students per class with 12:1 student-teacher ratio overall.

When should I apply?

Applications accepted year-round, but apply by February for September start. Waitlists exist.
